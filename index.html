Our Technology

How it works

CRISPR-Cas

Curacle employs CRISPR, a novel gene editing technology, to target and modify undesirable or promblem-causing genes in the cellular genome.

DNA Origami

DNA origami is used as a vehicle to deliver CRISPR in the body. Using CanDo by MIT, Curacle designs 3D bio-responsive DNA nanostructures with different receptors, specific to various cell types that enable optimal transition from the digestive system to the blood stream.

Leukocyte Membrane

Curacle uses state-of-the-art gesicle technology to coat the DNA origami with White Blood Cell membrane. By camouflaging the DNA nanostructure, we effectively prevent it from being rejected by the immune system and allow it to safely circulate the human body.

Case Study

Immuno-oncology Therapeutics


Aging leads to the deterioration of the human body immune system, making it more susceptible to the deadliest diseases in the world, such as cancer, cardiovascular diseases and neurodegeneration. By supplementing the body with CRISPR-mediated, synthetic white blood cells, Curacle optimizes the body's ability to fight the most fatal diseases.

Esophageal Cancer

Esophageal cancer is cancer that occurs in the esophagus — a long, hollow tube that runs from your throat to your stomach - and is the sixth most common cause of cancer deaths worldwide.CRISPR is able to knock out a gene in the T cells known as PD-1. The deleted genes reprogram the T cells to better find and attack cancer.

Leukemia

Leukemia is an aggressive blood cancer that can affect people of all ages. It develops in the bone marrow, overwhelming the healthy cells that reside there, which impairs the immune system leading to serious infections and bleeding. CRISPR enables the inhibition of METTL3 gene, which pushes the blood cancer cells towards apoptosis.

Crohn's Disease

Crohn’s disease is a chronic inflammatory condition of the gastrointestinal tract that causes severe swelling and pain. CRISPR can be employed to switch on the IL2RA gene, enabling the T cells to suppress inflammation and counteract the effects of Crohn's and inflammatory bowel disease.
